India's pharmaceutical industry relies on a range of tablet testing instruments to ensure product quality and regulatory compliance.

## Essential Tablet Testing Equipment

### Tablet Hardness Testers

Measure the mechanical strength of tablets:

- Manual and automatic options
- Load cell technology for precision
- Digital displays and data logging

### Tablet Friability Testers

Assess tablet durability:

- Single and multi-drum configurations
- USP compliant designs
- Programmable rotation counts

### Tablet Disintegration Testers

Determine disintegration time:

- Multiple station options
- Temperature controlled baths
- Automatic endpoint detection

### Dissolution Testers

Measure drug release rates:

- USP apparatus types 1, 2, 5, 6
- Automated sampling options
- Integrated analysis systems
